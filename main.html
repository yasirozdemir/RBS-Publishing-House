Information About Us

LitSEa is a publishing house focused on the translation of 'small literatures' from the Southeast Europe. The main objective of LitSEa is to challenge existing stereotypes about the literature from the region and contribute to building a more inclusive world literature scene. To achieve that objective, our publishing house focuses on the translation of both the works of emerging and established writers from the local languages (Croatian, Serbian, Bosnian, Slovenian) to the most represented European languages (German, French, Italian, English).

Poems

Pripadnost

Sunce,

čije si ti ime?

Kojoj galaksiji,

kojoj naciji,

kojem narodu pripadaš?

Pa ne možeš ti tako

po svijetu hodati:

danas na istoku,

sutra na zapadu.

Ti moraš da se odlučiš

čije si?

Da se izjasniš

konačno.

Mi to moramo znati:

u slučaju tvoje smrti

gdje ćemo te pokopati?

U kojoj zemlji?

U kojem kraju?

U kojoj četvrti?

Enes Kisević
Belonging

Sun, whose name are you?

Which Galaxy,

which Nation,

which People do you belong to ?,


Well, you really cannot go on

roaming like this around the world :

today in the East,

and tomorrow the West!


You have to make up your mind,

And finally declare yourself:

Whose are you?


We need to know:

In case you die,

where are we to burry you?


In which country,

in which district,

in which neighbourhood?

Translation: Svetlana Spaic
